Figure Creation and Retrieval Functions

The following functions create and retrieve figures (both shapes and instances, including mosaics) from the database.

Supported functions include:

  • dbCopyFig
  • dbMoveFig
  • dbGetOverlaps
  • dbGetTrueOverlaps
  • dbGetNeighbor
  • dbGetNeighborList
  • dbGetMaxHierDepth
  • dbGetPathSegStyle
  • dbSetPathSegStyle
  • dbGetPathBoundary
  • dbCheckParentChild
  • dbProduceOverlap
  • dbProduceOverlapInst
