Bounding Box Functions

A bounding box has several points. The figure shows bounding box points for a Polygon.

You can abbreviate bounding box point names as follows:

upperLeft

or

uL

lowerLeft

or

lL

upperCenter

or

uC

lowerCenter

or

lC

upperRight

or

uR

lowerRight

or

lR

centerLeft

or

cL

centerCenter

or

cC

centerRight

or

cR

This topic provides a list of Cadence® SKILL functions associated with bounding box points.

Only the functions listed here are supported for public use. All other functions, regardless of their name or prefix, and undocumented aspects of the functions described below, are private and are subject to change at anytime.
