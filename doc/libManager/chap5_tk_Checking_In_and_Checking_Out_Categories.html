Checking In and Checking Out Categories

To check in or check out a category, follow these steps:

  1. Make sure you can see the category names.
  2. Right-click a category name.
    A pop-up menu appears.
  3. Choose Check In to check in a category, or Check Out to check out a category.
    The files associated with the selected category appear on the form.
  4. Click OK.
