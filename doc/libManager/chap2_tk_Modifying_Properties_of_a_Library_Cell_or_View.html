Modifying Properties of a Library, Cell, or View

You can modify properties of a library, cell, or view from the Library/Cell/View Property Editor form that appears when you choose Edit – Properties.

To modify properties:

  1. On the Library/Cell/View Property Editor form, click Modify.
    The Modify ‘propertyName’ form appears.
    The name of the selected property appears in the title of the form.
  2. Make your desired changes on the form.
  3. Click OK.
