Sorting Cells in Abstract Generator

The Sort Cells form sorts the selected cells according to a variety of sorting criteria. The specified criteria are applied only to the selected cells displayed in the Cell pane and not to the overall library.

  1. Choose Cells – Sort command to open the Sort Cells form.
  2. In Method, select criterion for sorting from None, Name, Height, Status, or Selection.
  3. In View, select from Layout, Logical, Pins, Extract, or Abstract.
    The Height and Status methods consider only the view specified here. Cells that do not have the specified view are not sorted.
  4. In Order, select Ascending or Descending.
  5. Click OK to sort the cells when you have specified your criteria.
