Editing a Design Intent

You can edit a design intent using the Editing Design Intent form. Access the form by right-clicking the required object in the Navigator or on the canvas:

  • Right-click the required object and choose Design Intent – Edit Design Intent option.
  • Click the glyph of the object and click Edit on the Design Intent Info Balloon that is displayed.
  • Click the glyph of the object and double-click on the Design Intent Info Balloon that is displayed.

If an object is associated with more than one design intent, a submenu is displayed listing the different design intents available to be edited.
