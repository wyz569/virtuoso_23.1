Recalling a Design Partition

To recall a design partition submitted for merge:

  1. Click the Recall button on the Concurrent Layout toolbar in the assistant or choose Concurrent – Submit for Merge / Recall.
  2. In the Recall Submission dialog box, click OK.
    The design partition will be recalled and the status will change to Editing.
    You can recall a design partition only if the manager has not yet merged it.
