Information Pane

The information pane shows additional details about the changes on a selected object. For example, if an object is moved then the change type is Physical, or if it is assigned to a net then the type is Logical. In case the change causing an alert, the additional information lets you identify the issues with your edits and then take necessary steps to resolve them.
