Concurrent Layout Editing for Hierarchical Designs

Hierarchical editing in the CLE flow lets multiple designers concurrently Edit In Place (EIP) into hierarchical subcells. The edit scope is retained in both area and layer-based design partitions. In area-based design partitions, area boundaries defined at the top design partition are pushed down the hierarchy. In layer-based design partitions, edit scope is limited to the layers available in the top design partition.
