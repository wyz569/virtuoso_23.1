What is the purpose of Edit-in-Concert mode?

Edit-in-Concert mode lets you view and edit die packages and their corresponding die layouts synchronously. The modifications you make to a die are auto-updated on the other tabs in the background and are immediately visible on all the relevant tabs.

For example:

  • A bump moved in a die results in the movement of the bump at all locations in which the master bump cell has been instantiated.
  • A die moved in the package view results in its synchronous movement on the other tabs. 
  • Die instances and IO pads moved in a die results in synchronous movement of the die instances and IO pads on other tabs. 
  • Changes made to the Mirrored, Flipped, and Shrink Factor TILP parameters are propagated automatically to other tabs.
