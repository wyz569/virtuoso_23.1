Why does Power Manager require device registration?

The device registration information is used by Power Manager to read the device type and its topology in the design. For devices with more than two terminals, you can associate terminals to have specific mapping. Correct recognition and identification of device type is important for the supply traversal to find the related supplies of the boundary ports associated with the design logic.
