How can I map generic device terminal names in my PDK?

If, you do not have generic device terminal names in your PDK, map the terminal names to the corresponding terminal type for your PDK devices. This is required by Power Manager to read and perform the supply tracing accordingly.

The information can be provided in the Terminals section of the Devices tab in Virtuoso Power Manager Setup form as follows:

  1. Select the device by supplying the library information. When the device terminals are selected, the terminals appear in the Terminals section.
  2. Select a terminal and map it to the corresponding terminal type on the right.
