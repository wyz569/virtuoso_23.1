How do I register a three-terminal resistor as a connector?

You can register a three-terminal resistor as a connector on the Devices tab of the setup form. When the extractor traces the power to the ground paths in order to pair power and ground nets, if there is a connector instance in the path, the related terminals of the registered connector instance (a multi-terminal cell) help to continue tracing from one end to the other end.
