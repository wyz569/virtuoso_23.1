Splash Screen Functions

This topic provides a list of Cadence® SKILL functions associated with splash screens modification and windows. You can change the background, icon, and family and product names on a splash screen or About window.

These functions only apply to the Virtuoso Studio design environment.

Only the functions listed here are supported for public use. All other functions, regardless of their name or prefix, and undocumented aspects of the functions described below, are private and are subject to change at any time.

  • hiSetSplashDefaultBackground
  • hiSetSplashIcon
  • hiSetSplashFamily
